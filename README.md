# Totem Indicator

The mod adds a pulsing outline around the edge of your screen when you're taking damage. It helps newer players stay aware of what's happening without having to constantly look at their health bar, which is useful when you're still getting used to the pace of crystal pvp.
Crystal pvp moves fast. There's a lot going on at once. You're placing crystals, breaking them, watching your opponent, and trying not to take too much damage all at the same time. For someone just starting out, it's easy to lose track of your health in the middle of all that. That's where this mod helps.

Usage:

```
/totemindicator toggle
```
